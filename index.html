Hey! I am Jerrin Bright, currently working at the Indian Institute of Science (IISc) on adaptive obstacle avoidance for UAVs in cluttered environments. I will be graduating from Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT), India with a Bachelor's at Mechanical Engineering. My undergraduate research primarily focussed on End-to-End Autonomy for UAVs in unstructured and unknown environments.

Hard work being my core pillar, combined with practical experiences, I am someone you could count on to persevere through challenges. I am an ardent follower of cricket, chess and loves travelling.
