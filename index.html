My ultimate vision is to enhance the autonomy of visual sensors to aid in unbiased performance indication and action recognition specifically in sports via analysing the posture of the players. Thus, I am working as a researcher at the Sports Analytics Research Group (SARG), part of the Vision & Image Processing Lab under the guidance of Dr. John S Zelek and Dr. David A Clausi. My current work is towards improving the visual understanding of human postures using a monocular vision sensor specifically for sports analysis (baseball and ice‑hockey) using transformer-based approaches leveraging high resolution 2D poses estimated using CNN.

As an undergraduate, at the Vellore Institute of Technology, I worked on end-to-end autonomous navigation of UAVs in unstructured and GPS denied environments leveraging learning approaches and cutting-edge deep learning algorithms. I am an avid cricket and chess enthusiast who also enjoys travelling and cycling.
