My ultimate vision is to enhance the autonomy of machines to aid human society. In particular, I believe that a robot's physical intelligence is the key to automate many inhumane physical labors. Thus, as a researcher at VIP Lab, I will be working towards enhancing the visual understanding of robots for social tasks using semantic modeling and 3D mapping.

As an undergraduate, at the Vellore Institute of Technology, I worked on end-to-end autonomous navigation of UAVs in unstructured and GPS denied environments leveraging learning approaches and cutting-edge deep learning algorithms. I am an avid cricket and chess enthusiast who also enjoys travelling and cycling.

Core Interests:

  •   Autonomous Navigation
